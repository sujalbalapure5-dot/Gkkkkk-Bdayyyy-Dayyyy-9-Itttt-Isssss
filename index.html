DAY 9 / 10

In Every Universe,
I'd Still Find You ❤️

I genuinely think some souls are written for each other long before they meet. Because no matter how many different lives I imagine... different cities, different timings, different worlds, different versions of us... somehow my heart still reaches yours. Again. And again. And again.

🌧 Universe 1 — Strangers In A Rainy City
Maybe in another universe we never meet the way we did here. No same paths. No same people. No same timing.

Maybe you are living in another city completely. And one random evening while it's raining heavily outside... I walk into a small bookstore just to escape the rain.

And there you are. Standing near the window. Adjusting your specs while reading something seriously. Completely unaware that someone across the room is about to unknowingly fall in love with your existence.

I don't know your name yet. You don't know mine. But something inside me still pauses. Like my soul recognizing something before my mind understands it.

And maybe I still keep staring too long. Maybe you still catch me staring. Maybe you still try to act serious while secretly smiling. And maybe somehow... even in that universe... we still slowly become each other's favorite person.
✉ Universe 2 — A World Without Phones
Maybe there is another lifetime where no phones exist. No Instagram. No reels. No late-night typing. No video calls. No notifications carrying your name.

Just letters. Slow waiting. Ink carrying emotions across cities.

And honestly baby... I know myself enough to know this: I would still write to you endlessly. Pages and pages filled with overthinking, love, jealousy, comfort, stupid flirting, emotional honesty, and the million tiny thoughts my mind creates about you every day.

And every time your letter arrives... I would still smile before even opening it. Still hold it close to my chest first. Still reread your words at night when missing you becomes unbearable.

Because it was never technology that connected me to you. It was always you. Always your soul.
🫀 Universe 3 — Loving Through Difficult Times
Maybe somewhere there is a universe where life becomes extremely difficult for us. Dreams fail. Plans break. The world becomes emotionally exhausting.

And maybe in that universe too... you still quietly carry pain without fully expressing it. You still pretend you're okay first. You still hide tears behind normal conversations. And I still notice. Because loving you was never surface-level for me.

I notice the silence in your voice. The heaviness in your texts. The way your energy changes when your heart is hurting.

And maybe in that universe too... I still sit beside you during the difficult nights. Still reminding you to stay. Still listening. Still choosing you even on emotionally messy days.

Because baby... I don't think my soul knows how to leave yours halfway.
⏳ Universe 4 — Growing Old Together
And maybe my favorite universe of all... is the one where we grow old together.

White hair. Wrinkles. Slow walks. Medicines beside the bed. Your specs still sliding down your nose while reading something seriously. Me still staring at you instead of listening properly.

You still finding double meanings in normal conversations. Still laughing the same way. Still getting annoyed at me for small things. Still stealing blanket at night.

And maybe one evening we sit quietly together watching the sky. No noise. No rush. No pressure. Just us. After surviving years and years of life together.

And maybe I still randomly look at you after all those years and think: “God... I am still completely in love with this girl.”

And honestly? I think that is the kind of love I want most. Not temporary excitement. Not perfect fantasy. Just a love that survives time itself.
❤️ Even If...
Even if life becomes difficult... I would still choose you.

Even if distance happens sometimes... I would still choose you.

Even if misunderstandings hurt us... I would still choose you.

Even if we become emotionally tired... I would still choose you.

Even if the world changes completely around us... my heart would still search for yours first.

Because this love was never built only on easy moments. It was built from comfort. Attachment. Late-night honesty. Patience. Healing. Friendship. Pain. Understanding. And thousands of tiny moments that slowly became my emotional home.
You know what genuinely scares me sometimes? The fact that I cannot imagine a version of life where meeting you didn't matter deeply.

You affected me too permanently. Too emotionally. Too beautifully.

And maybe that's why this love feels different from everything else. Because it doesn't feel temporary. It feels written somewhere long before us. Like something the universe quietly planned before we even existed.

Sometimes I think souls like ours are meant to continuously find each other. In every timeline. In every universe. In every possible version of life.

And honestly baby... if I had to relive every difficult moment again, every misunderstanding, every emotional night, every overthinking spiral, every painful conversation... but the reward at the end was still “you”...

I would still do it all over again. Without hesitation. Without fear. Without changing a thing.

Because at the end of every version of my life... every road somehow still leads me back to you.

And maybe that is what real love actually is. Not perfection. Not fantasy. Not temporary attraction.

Just two souls continuously choosing each other... even when life becomes complicated. Even when emotions become heavy. Even when the world becomes difficult.

And baby... if there are truly infinite universes out there somewhere... then I genuinely believe one thing with my whole heart:

In every single one of them... I would still search for you. Still fall for you. Still stay. Still love you.

Always you. ❤️
